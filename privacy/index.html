Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-24. This policy explains how Axial handles user data.

Home Terms of Use Contact Support

We Don't Collect Your Data

All data you enter in Axial stays on your device and in your personal iCloud account. We never see, access, or collect information about you or your children.

iCloud Sync

If you enable iCloud, your data syncs through Apple's iCloud infrastructure. Apple manages that data according to its own privacy policy. We do not have access to it.

No Analytics

We do not use third-party analytics, crash reporting, advertising SDKs, or tracking services.

No Third Parties

We do not share personal or health-related data with third parties because we do not collect your data in the first place.

Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time by removing child profiles in the app or by deleting the app from your device. iCloud-synced data will be removed according to Apple's sync behavior.
